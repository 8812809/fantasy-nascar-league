League Tradition

What began as a private fantasy NASCAR competition has grown into a long-running league built around weekly racing, friendly competition, team management, and bragging rights.

Each segment gives owners a fresh chance to draft drivers, follow the NASCAR schedule, chase weekly side-bet wins, and compete for the segment championship.

Current Format

The league currently uses a segment-based format with team rosters, weekly race scoring, weekly side-bet winners, and Segment Points tracking throughout each segment.
