League Structure

The league consists of 10 teams. Each team has 4 NASCAR Cup Series cars/drivers. The league is divided into 3 segments of 12 races each, with a redraft after each segment.

Scoring

Qualifying Points

  • 1st = 40 points
  • 2nd = 39 points
  • 3rd = 38 points
  • Points decrease by 1 per position through 40th = 1 point.

Race Finish Points

  • 1st = 120 points
  • 2nd = 117 points
  • 3rd = 114 points
  • Points decrease by 3 per position through 40th = 3 points.

Team Score

A team's weekly score consists of the combined qualifying points and race points earned by all four of its active drivers/cars.

Weekly Side Bet

The team with the highest weekly score for a race wins the weekly side bet.

Driver Transactions

  • Each team is allowed one car change per segment.
  • No car change may be made until after the first race of the segment is completed.
  • Any car change must be completed before qualifying begins to be included in that race.
  • Points earned by the dropped car remain with that team.
  • Points earned by the newly added car begin when that car becomes active on the roster.
  • Once a team uses its one car change, that team is roster locked until the next segment draft.

Roster Status

🔓 Eligible: Team has not used its car change for the segment.

🔒 Locked: Team has already used its car change for the segment.

Commissioner Authority

The commissioner has final authority on scoring disputes, roster issues, corrections, and rule interpretations.
