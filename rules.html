League Rules

  • 10 teams.
  • Each team drafts 4 cars.
  • 3 segments of 12 races each.
  • Complete redraft after each segment.
  • A driver may be owned by up to 2 teams.
  • Segment champion is most points over 12 races.
  • Weekly side bet goes to highest weekly score.
