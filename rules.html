1. LEAGUE FORMAT

  • 10 Teams
  • 4 Car Numbers Per Team
  • 3 Segments Per Season
  • 12 Races Per Segment
  • Complete Redraft After Each Segment

3. WEEKLY SIDE-BET

  • Highest team score wins the weekly side-bet.
  • No money amounts are displayed on the website.

4. SEGMENT CHAMPION

  • Most cumulative points after 12 races wins the segment.
  • No tiebreaker.

5. CAR OWNERSHIP

  • Teams draft and own car numbers, not drivers.
  • Points earned by a car belong to the team that owns that car number.
  • If a different driver competes in that car, ownership remains unchanged.
  • A car number may be owned by a maximum of two teams.
  • Car ownership remains in effect for the segment unless a team uses its one permitted Car Number Change.

6. CAR NUMBER CHANGES

  • Each team is allowed one Car Number Change per segment.
  • No changes are permitted until after the first race of the segment has been completed.
  • All changes must be completed before qualifying begins for the next race.
  • The replacement car number must comply with all ownership rules.
  • Once a team uses its one Car Number Change, that team's roster is locked for the remainder of the segment.
Example: After Race 1, a team may replace one car number before qualifying for Race 2. Once that change has been used, that team's roster is locked for the remainder of the segment.

7. COMMISSIONER DECISIONS

  • Official NASCAR results are used for all scoring.
  • Any scoring corrections will be based on official NASCAR data.
  • Commissioner decisions are final.
