League Structure

The Fantasy NASCAR League has 10 teams. Each team carries 4 drivers/cars per segment.

Segment Format

The league is divided into 3 segments of 12 races each. A full redraft is held after each segment.

Scoring System

Qualifying/start points run from 1st = 40 points down to 40th = 1 point. Race finish points run from 1st = 120 points down to 40th = 4 points.

Team weekly scores are calculated from the 4 active cars on that team's roster.

Weekly Side Bet

The team with the highest weekly score wins the weekly side bet.

Segment Champion

The team with the most points over the 12-race segment is the Segment Champion. No tiebreaker rules are used.

Driver Transactions

  1. Each team gets one car change per segment.
  2. No team may make a car change until after the first race of the segment is completed.
  3. Any change must be completed before qualifying begins to be included in that race.
  4. All points earned by the dropped car remain with that team.
  5. The newly added car begins earning points for that team once active.
  6. After a team uses its one transaction, that team is roster locked until the next segment draft.

Roster Status

🔓 Eligible: Team has not used its one transaction for the segment.

🔒 Locked: Team has used its one transaction and cannot make another change until the next segment draft.

Commissioner Authority

The commissioner has final authority on scoring disputes, roster issues, corrections, and rule interpretations.

Website and Standings

The website is the official league record. Corrections may be made by the commissioner if scoring or roster errors are found.
